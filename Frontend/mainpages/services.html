Admin Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard in an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system is a powerful tool designed for enterprise managers to oversee and manage various aspects of the business. This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee details, inventory or product information, sales and purchase data, and financial performance, enabling managers to make informed decisions and optimize operations. Manage purchase orders, track supplier information, monitor purchase history, and ensure timely procurement of goods. Customize the dashboard to display the most relevant and critical information through widgets and reports. Receive real-time notifications and alerts for important events such as low inventory levels, pending approvals, and financial anomalies.

Employee Dashboard

The Employee Dashboard in an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system is a centralized portal where employees can access their personal and work-related information. By logging in with their unique ID and password, employees can view their attendance records, leave balances, and assigned tasks, promoting transparency and self-service. View and update personal information such as contact details, emergency contacts, and more. Download attendance, leave, and task reports for personal records or review.

Attendance Management

An Attendance Tracker within an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system is essential for monitoring employee attendance, managing leave, and ensuring accurate payroll processing. It helps organizations keep track of when employees are present, absent, or on leave, providing valuable data for workforce management and planning. Automatically record and report absences, ensuring accurate tracking of employee attendance. Record and calculate overtime hours worked, facilitating correct compensation.

Task Management

A Task Manager within an ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system is a critical tool for managing and monitoring tasks, projects, and workflows efficiently. It helps streamline processes, ensures accountability, and enhances productivity across the organization. Monitor the progress of each task with status indicators such as 'Not Started', 'In Progress', 'Completed', or 'On Hold'. Set due dates for tasks and receive reminders to ensure timely completion.
